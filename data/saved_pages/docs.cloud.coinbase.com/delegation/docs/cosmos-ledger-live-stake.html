2: Stake to Cloud with Ledger Live

1. Delegate your ATOM â€‹

  1. Select Accounts within Ledger Live and open your Cosmos account.

  2. Under Delegation(s) on the Cosmos account dashboard select Earn Rewards .

  3. On the pop-up screen, select Continue .

  4. On the Validators screen, click in the search box to Search by name or address...


Coinbase Cloud Validator Information

Validator name : Coinbase Cloud Validator group address: cosmosvaloper1crqm3598z6qmyn2kkcl9dz7uqs4qdqnr6s8jdn

  1. Enter Coinbase Cloud's validator address: cosmosvaloper1crqm3598z6qmyn2kkcl9dz7uqs4qdqnr6s8jdn .

  2. To the right of the estimated yield for the Coinbase Cloud validator, enter the amount of ATOM you would like to delegate and click Continue .

  3. Confirm that the validator address is correct on the screen of your Ledger hardware device, and, if so, select to confirm.

A verification page will show that your delegation is complete.

Congratulations! You are now staked to a Coinbase Cloud validator.
